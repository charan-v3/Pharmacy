Our History

Established in 2004, Pharmacy+ has been serving the Namakkal community for 20 years. Founded by Suresh M, our pharmacy has grown from a small storefront to a trusted healthcare destination known for its commitment to excellence and compassionate service.

Our Service

At Pharmacy+, we offer a comprehensive range of pharmacy services designed to meet the diverse needs of our customers. From prescription filling and medication therapy management to immunizations and specialty compounding, we are dedicated to providing personalized care and innovative solutions that enhance the health and well-being of our community.

Quality and Safety

We adhere to the highest standards of quality and safety in all aspects of our operations. Our pharmacy is fully licensed and accredited, and our team follows strict protocols to ensure accurate medication dispensing, proper storage, and compliance with all regulatory requirements. Your health and safety are our top priorities.
